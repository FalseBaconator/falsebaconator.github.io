Delver

Made in C#

Delver is a top down dungeon exploration game programmed in C#. This was made in my first year of college. I was tasked with making a topdown game where you attacked by walking into enemies, I decided to go beyond that and explore procedural dungeon generation. The game was later modified by a classmate of mine who added a shop system and a quest system.

What I Did

I was the primary developer for Delver, with a couple additions made by a classmate much later.

  • Designed and implemented a random dungeon generation system.
  • Created an enemy manager and a modular enemy design to allow for easy expansion.
  • Designed and implemented a minimap that fills in as you explore the dungeon.
