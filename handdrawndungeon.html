Hand Drawn Dungeon

Hand Drawn Dungeon is a game wherein you control three party members as they go through multiple randomly generated combat encounters. I made it for a school project, and was the only one working on it. The intention was to make a lighthearted and casual game with just enough strategic potential to keep the player engaged.

My Role: Lead Developer, Lead Programmer, Lead Artist

I was the only one who worked on this project, so I fulfilled all roles needed for it.

  • I designed the concept, gameplay, and artstyle.
  • I created the player upgrade, encounter generation, and combat systems.
  • I made sound effects using jsfxr and searched for suitable background music on pixabay.
  • I created a tutorial and adjusted the difficulty based on feedback recieved during playtesting.
