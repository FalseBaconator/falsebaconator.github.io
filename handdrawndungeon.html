Hand Drawn Dungeon

Made in Unity

Hand Drawn Dungeon is dungeon crawler game that has the player control three party members as they go through multiple randomly generated combat encounters. I was the only one who worked on it. It's a lighthearted and casual game with just enough strategic potential to keep the player engaged.

you can play it on itch.io

What I Did

I made everything but the music, which I found online on Pixabay and were made by a user named Guilherme Bernades.

  • Designed the game.
  • Created the combat, encounter generation, and party upgrade systems.
  • Drew all the art.
  • Made sound effects using jsfxr.
  • Created a tutorial and adjusted the difficulty based on feedback recieved during playtesting.

    • Encounter Generation

    While boss encounters are predetermined, every other encounter is procedurally generated. When the player gets to the doors screen, two potential encounters are created. The general process of encounter generation is as follows:

    1. Randomly decide how many enemies there are in the encounter between 1 and 3.
    2. Decide which enemies are in the encounter.
    3. Determine a danger rating based off of the enemies present.
    4. Present two doors with danger ratings to the player so they can decide to play it safe or go for more XP.
